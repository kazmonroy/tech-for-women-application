Why This Course

Diversity and inclusion are among the biggest challenges in the tech industry today. It is evident that there are many successful and respected male software engineers as the industry has long been male-dominated. I envision a world where women and non-binaries could be proportionally represented as recognized software engineers, technical leaders and role models. However, it is crucial to act and be the change we want to see in the world. Therefore, the IT – Tech for Women course is the right place to start to be part of this change.

I am excited to study this course because I want to pursue a career in Web Development and narrow the gender gap in the tech industry. I enjoy learning new things and challenge myself. In fact, I started self-learning web development this year and discovered that Web Development pushes all the buttons for my passions. It brings together creativity and problem solving to build web applications with a purpose to make a positive impact in people's lives.

In addition, I look forward to network with aspiring female developers like me and be inspired by female guest speakers. I want to upskill and expand my knowledge through this course as it covers modern web technologies and tools such as JavaScript, React and Figma, which are essential to build web pages and web apps. I enjoy learning by doing and look forward to learn together with other like-minded female classmates who also seek to achieve diversity in Tech.

Future Developer

I think I would be a great future developer because I believe being a web developer is more than just programming and building beautiful websites, it is an opportunity to make a positive impact and help people through the power of technology. As an aspiring future developer, I want to build websites and web applications with a purpose, something that people can use every day and make their lives better. Web development in particular has enabled society to utilize the magic of the internet and solve many of everyday problems.

In addition, I want to influence change and make the world a more inclusive and safer place. As a web developer, it is always important to put user's experience first regardless of any physical condition. Therefore, it is imperative to consider web accessibility best practices when developing a new website. Moreover, in today's global “data economy”, it is crucial to make secure websites and apps to protect user's personal information and be transparent about the data we gather to deliver a better personalized experience.

Building a website or a web app is much more than a series of well-designed elements and technologies bundled up together, it reflects who I am and what positive outcome I can bring to the world with all these technologies.

About Me

Hej! My name is Katherine Monroy. I come from Ecuador but I currently live in Malmö. I came to Sweden about 4 years ago to do my Master's degree in International Business Strategy at Linnaeus University in Kalmar. However, I am pursuing to change careers and become a Web Developer because it would give me the possibility to build web applications with a purpose to make a positive impact in people's lives.

I embrace change as an opportunity to learn and grow. Especially now that I wish to switch careers. Previously, I worked in Marketing for about 7 years. I worked with brands that stood for a purpose and made a positive change. However, I want to do more than marketing. I yearn to learn more, something different but alike. As with brands, the web is about creating meaningful and human connections in a creative way. Therefore, I became interest in Web Development as it gives me the possibility to be creative and craft my ideas by building websites and web apps to allow people connect and make technology accessible for everybody.

In addition, I am a driven person who enjoys learning and overcoming challenges. I am also a positive person who sees failures as lessons and motivations to improve. I enjoy traveling. I have always seen myself as a citizen of the world and I have been lucky enough to see it and meet new people from different backgrounds. This experience has allowed me to learn about different cultures and languages, embrace diversity but more importantly believe that combining ideas, especially within a diverse team, is the best way to innovate and create magical outcomes.

I am thrilled to embark into this new Web Development career journey. I believe it is never too late to change one's story and I feel Sundsgården Folkhögskola is the best place to start.
